The Engine Room

ORDER MANAGEMENT CORE WORKFLOW

Minimum Manual Touch is one of the department's most critical KPIs — minimizing manual order entry, reducing manual product changes, and maximizing the share of orders flowing automatically through EDI. Every manual step increases the risk of error and extends the overall operation time.

For MT customers, order days are Monday and Thursday with a strict 15:00 cut-off for order acceptance — a deadline shaped by the full downstream chain: SAP transfer, credit control, Finance release, Demand Planning review, delivery creation, and the end-of-day delivery report to Logistics.

Orders that don't arrive via EDI are entered through the Excel Upload macro, requiring precise entry of the Ambient/TC flag, correct warehouse, accurate customer information and correct product codes.
